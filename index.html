Talk 2 Me

Talk 2 Me is a website designed by us for YOU.

47% of immigrants in the US report a limited English proficiency, lowering their chances at securing a future, having a social life, or comfortably assimilating into society. We aim to make the daunting challenge of learning a new language easier by taking it one step at a time- word by word.

Click "Word" to enter a sentence in your native language, convert it to English to learn a new sentence every day. Click "Synonym" to find similar words to an English word you enter to expand your vocabulary.
